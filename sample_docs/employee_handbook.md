# Employee Handbook (Excerpt)

## Paid Time Off

All full-time employees are entitled to fifteen (15) days of paid time
off per calendar year. PTO accrues monthly and is available for use
immediately upon hire. Unused PTO carry-over into the next year is
capped at five (5) days; any balance above that cap is forfeited on
January 1st.

## Sick Leave

Employees receive ten (10) days of paid sick leave per year. Sick leave
carries over without limit and may be used for the employee's own
illness or to care for an immediate family member.

## Parental Leave

Eligible employees are entitled to twelve (12) weeks of fully paid
parental leave following the birth or adoption of a child. Eligibility
begins after ninety (90) days of employment.

## Bereavement Leave

In the event of the death of an immediate family member, employees are
entitled to five (5) days of paid bereavement leave. Extended bereavement
leave may be requested through HR.

## Remote Work

Employees may work remotely up to three (3) days per week with manager
approval. Fully remote arrangements require approval from the department
head and HR.
