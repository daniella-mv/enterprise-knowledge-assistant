# Information Security Policy (Excerpt)

## Passwords

All employee account passwords must be at least twelve (12) characters
in length and contain at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase
letter, one number, and one special character. Passwords must be
rotated every ninety (90) days. Reuse of any of the previous five (5)
passwords is prohibited.

## Multi-Factor Authentication

Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is **required** for all access to
production systems, email, and any service that handles customer data.
Authenticator apps (such as Google Authenticator or Duo) are required;
SMS-based MFA is no longer accepted.

## Remote Access

All remote access to corporate systems must occur through the company
VPN. The approved VPN client is Cisco AnyConnect. Connecting from
public Wi-Fi without VPN is prohibited.

## Data Classification

Company data is classified into four levels:

- **Public** — may be freely shared (e.g., marketing materials)
- **Internal** — for employees only (e.g., org charts)
- **Confidential** — restricted to authorized employees (e.g., contracts)
- **Restricted** — strictly controlled (e.g., customer PII, source code)

## Incident Reporting

Suspected security incidents must be reported to security@company.com
within one (1) hour of discovery. After-hours incidents should also be
escalated by phone to the on-call security engineer.
